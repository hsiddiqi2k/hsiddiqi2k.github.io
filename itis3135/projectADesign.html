Project A Webpage Design

Project Overview

  • My application is web-based application for my younger sister so that she may create an art portfolio online
  • The intented users for this site are people looking to hire my sister for freelance art jobs
  • The site will contain a total of 5 pages, which will include a home page with noteworth art pieces, a gallery with all art, a place where orders can be placed, a contact information page, and a page showing upcoming art events where she will be attending

Client Information

  • Client Name: Aisha Siddiqi
  • She is currently a student at UNCC in her sophomore year

Wireframe

  • It was requested that the main landing page show only the most prominent pieces of art, as for an artist it is a good first impression

Sitemap

Page Design

  • Home
  • The purpose of this page is to show the most prominent art chosen by the artist
  • Audience of this site is people who want to see the chosen art of the artist
  • Content of this page will be 9 images chosen by the client, with the name at the top left and quick navigation at the top right
  • No data will be entered on this page
  • There will be hyperlinks but no dropdowns
  • Actions on this page include navigation to other pages


  • Prints
  • The purpose of this page is to act as a portfolio for the client, showing all art created
  • Audience of this site is people who might want to send a commission to the artist, but wish to see more work
  • Content of this page will be all art created by the artist, with the name at the top left and quick navigation at the top right
  • No data will be entered on this page
  • There will be hyperlinks but no dropdowns
  • Actions on this page include navigation to other pages


  • Commisions
  • The purpose of this page to have clients send a commission to my client
  • Audience of this site is people who have decided to send a commission t
  • Content of this page will be a where clients can enter information required to send a commission
  • Users will be asked to enter data on this page
  • Data will require validation
  • There will be hyperlinks and dropdowns
  • Actions on this page include navigation to other pages, and data being proccessed when filling in textboxes and clicking buttons


  • Contact
  • The purpose of this page is to provide people with information required to contact my client
  • The audience of this page is people wanting to contact my client
  • Content of this page will be the information required to contact my client
  • No data will be entered
  • There will be hyperlinks on this page
  • Actions on this page include navigation to other pages


  • Exhibits
  • The purpose of this page is to show upcoming art events where she will be attending
  • The audience of this page is people wanting to see where my client will be showing her art next
  • Content of this page will be a calender showing which days there will be art exhibits my client will be attending
  • No data will be entered
  • There will be hyperlinks on this page
  • Actions on this page include navigation to other pages

Dynamic Functionality

  • A photo gallery will be on the prints page
